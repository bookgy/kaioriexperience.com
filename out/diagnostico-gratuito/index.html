FAQ

Frequently asked questions

We answer the most common questions before you request your free diagnosis.

Does the free diagnosis have any cost?⌄

No. It is a first assessment with no cost and no obligation. Its goal is to understand your case, review initial signals and guide you on what type of analysis could help.

What exactly do I receive?⌄

You receive initial guidance on possible friction points detected in your digital presence, public information, decision process or customer experience. It is not a full report, but a first assessment to decide whether it makes sense to go deeper.

Does this replace a complete audit?⌄

No. The free diagnosis is the first step. A complete audit analyses the customer journey in depth, from search and booking to the real experience, departure and reviews.

Do I need to have a website?⌄

Not necessarily. We can review your Google profile, platform listings, social profiles or any channel where guests discover, compare or book your business.

What type of businesses is this for?⌄

It is designed for hotels, vacation rentals, restaurants, tours, experiences and service businesses where customer perception directly affects decisions, satisfaction and recommendation.

Do you also analyse reviews?⌄

Yes. In this first assessment we can review visible review signals and detect initial patterns. In a complete audit, reputation is analysed in more depth.

Can the diagnosis be for a business outside Spain?⌄

Yes. Kaiori can analyse hospitality and service businesses in different countries, especially when experience, booking and online reputation are key to attracting customers.

What happens after the diagnosis?⌄

If we see clear signals, we recommend the most suitable next step: digital audit, Mystery Guest, reputation analysis or a complete customer journey audit.

Do I need to share internal data?⌄

Not for this first assessment. We can start with public information: website, Google profile, platforms, social media and reviews. Internal data can enrich a deeper audit later.

Who will review my case?⌄

The Kaiori team will review your case from a real guest perspective and with a business focus. We do not analyse only aesthetics or communication: we look for signals that may affect trust, satisfaction and reputation.
